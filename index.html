Alright, so here's the deal. I love red pandas. This is not particularly a secret, and it's not very hard to see why. They're ridiculously cute, look very fluffy, and seem very playful.

They're also incredibly endangered in the wild.

To be fair, they're ridiculous animals from a zoological point of view. The red panda has a body plan designed for predation, but instead it eats mostly bamboo leaves.

Bamboo is hard to digest, has very little nutritional value (so you need to eat a LOT of it in a day), and covers a wide area for the the energy you get out of it. So it's really silly as a foodsource.
